Reaserch direction--Revolutionizing Cardiac Care: The Biodegradable Metal Cardiovascular Stent

The biodegradable metal cardiovascular stent is a groundbreaking device designed to treat blocked arteries and restore healthy blood flow to the heart. Unlike traditional stents that remain in the body permanently, this innovative stent is made from a special type of metal that gradually dissolves over time, leaving behind a healed artery. This means no long-term implant and potentially fewer complications for patients. It's a major step forward in cardiac care, offering a more natural and promising solution for heart health.
